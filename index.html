Hello there!

Welcome to N2S! This is a collection of open-source summaries with one goal: helping people understand complex topics in a way that is easier to grasp. Unfortunately, some topics are never easy (read: signal processing... ). Jokes aside, this summary is not meant to replace a good course, but rather to serve as a guide and resource in a not-too-short but also not-too-long format. I wanted to create something a bit more comprehensive than typical summaries, but of course, not as heavy as a textbook.

Please check out our repository and feel free to share any feedback or contributions that you think might be helpful to others.

My hope is that this becomes your go-to resource when you're trying to wrap your head around these concepts. And hey, if you've read this far, you're awesome! Thanks for being here, and let's learn some cool stuff together!
